About the Collective

There_They’re Collective is a cohort of creatives, outsider artists, and activists working to create and support the integration of political art within nightlife and queer spaces. We aim to foster more robust connections through interactive pieces and innovative social architecture/scenography.

We are motivated by a shared understanding of the imperative to harness the political potential of queer convening in nightlife and other spaces as constituents of the queer commons. We are committed to the democratization of art outside of traditional gallery spaces and hours and fostering accessible queer safe spaces within nightlife. Working primarily work in Beirut and Berlin, we prioritize building connections and durable collaborations with other creatives and outsider artists and collectives that share our values to create collaborative works in which skill sharing and multi-disciplinary expression are prioritised.

To learn more about our upcoming research, events, and installations, please reach out on the contact page.
