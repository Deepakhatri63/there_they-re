Group II Instructions

We are working to disrupt the shadowbans on the social media accounts of sex workers who have had their accounts deprioritised and restricted because of content they shared related to sex work & queer topics.

Step 1: To participate, please follow these accounts by manually entering their names on Instagram:

Kody.tm

Step 2: View their most recent two stories, like them, and send a positive emoji response. (Our team preselected these stories for how they will perform)

Step 3: Wait a few minutes or longer and return to the accounts.

Step 4: Like, save, and comment positively on their most recent reel post. Keep the post saved for at least 48 hours.

Step 5: Continue to follow this account for at least a few months, but longer is better. If you continue to engage with their posts and stories, that would be very helpful!

Thank you for participating in this initiative to push back against censorship of queer voices on social media, particularly the targeting of sex workers and activists. If you are interested in learning more about There_They’re, you can find more information in the About Us section of this website
